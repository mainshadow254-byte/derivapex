Getting started

1. Sign up & verify email

Create an account with email and password, then click the verification link we email you. Telegram is optional and can be finished later for alerts and community access.

2. Telegram setup

Telegram is optional. Open the ApexBot Telegram bot from onboarding or Account Settings if you want alerts and community access, verify your community join if required, or skip and continue using the dashboard.

3. Demo first

Free accounts get the demo scanner preview, demo trades, and live charts — clearly labeled DEMO. Nothing here uses real money.

4. Upgrade for real tools

Paid plans unlock the full AI scanner, copy trading, bot imports, and real trading via your own Deriv account. Subscriptions activate only after a verified payment.

Risk

Trading involves real financial risk. The AI shows confidence and risk warnings but never guarantees profit. Only trade what you can afford to lose.
