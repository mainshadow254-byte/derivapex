REPORT

What to send

Send the page name, exact error, whether you were in DEMO or REAL mode, and what action caused it.

Good report: “demo-scanner.html shows 425 Not enough candle data after selecting R_100.” Bad report: “it failed.”

Before contacting support

  1. Record the page and exact error message.
  2. State whether you were in DEMO or REAL mode.
  3. Never include passwords, tokens, API keys, payment secrets, or one-time codes.
  4. For financial risk concerns, stop trading first and review responsible-trading information.
