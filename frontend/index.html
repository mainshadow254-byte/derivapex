FAQ

Know what you are using

Are the free bots guaranteed to win?

No. They are learning templates for demo testing. You must edit, backtest, and risk-limit any strategy before considering real trading.

Does a successful backtest guarantee profit?

No. It is a historical simulation with stated assumptions. Live payouts, latency, and future conditions can differ.

Can ApexBot trade without my permission?

Real trading requires your connected Deriv account and backend authorization. Tokens are encrypted and never sent to the frontend.

Are marketplace statistics real?

Follower counts, reviews, installs, trades, profit, and drawdown should be calculated from backend records rather than browser storage.

Where should a beginner start?

Use the no-login demo, follow the guide, build a template, compare several backtests, and remain in demo mode until you understand the risk.
